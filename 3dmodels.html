Project Overview
Hundreds of stunning 3D models, only 3ds max and creativity - How long can you impress the world with your art? In 3D modeling and animation software “3ds max” your screen will be filled with realistic and immersive 3D models and animations! Grab your mouse and keyboard and prepare for endless hours of artistic fun!
I am a 3D artist with over five month of experience in creating realistic and immersive 3D models and animations using 3ds max. I have worked on various projects, such as video games, commercials, films, and architectural visualizations. Some of my notable works include a realistic character model for a horror game, a dynamic cityscape animation for a car commercial, a detailed interior design for a film set, and a photorealistic exterior rendering for a residential building. I am proficient in using 3ds max tools and features, such as modeling, texturing, lighting, rigging, animating, rendering, and post-processing. I am always eager to learn new skills and techniques to improve my craft and deliver high-quality results for my clients.
