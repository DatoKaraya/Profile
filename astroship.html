Astroship

Godot Game

Project Overview
Astroship is a 3D prototype of a top-down arcade space shooter. The game scene has the player's spaceship, asteroids, and enemy ships. The player's task is to destroy as many asteroids and enemy ships as possible.
Mechanics
The main mechanics of my game are the mechanics of the flight ship in 3d space, the mechanics of shooting and asteroid physics behavior in my game
Dynamics During the development of the prototype, I made a small emphasis on the visual component. When a player plays my game, he sees how his engines starts to burn, how his ship starts to move, how the light of his searchlights illuminates his path and passing by asteroids cast their shadow into the void. The player sees how his projectiles explode on impact with the enemy
Aesthetics From this point of view, I wanted to convey the feeling of the emptiness of space, the feeling of pitch darkness where only the light from the ship allows you to see what is happening in space
